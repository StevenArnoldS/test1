Build your best body

Push Up

Push-ups are a basic exercise that uses body weight to work the chest, triceps, and deltoids.
Sit Up

Sit-ups primarily target the abdominal muscles.
Pull Up

Pull-ups strengthen the upper back and arms by lifting your body until your chin passes a bar.
Squat

Squats are a strength exercise for the thighs, hips, buttocks, quadriceps, and hamstrings.
Plank

Planks are a stability exercise that trains the whole body, focusing especially on the core.
Bench Press

The bench press is a weight training exercise that targets the chest, triceps, and deltoids.
