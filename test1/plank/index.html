Plank

The plank exercise strengthens the core, shoulders, and arms. It requires maintaining a position similar to a push-up for the maximum possible time.

Steps to perform a Plank:

  1. Start in a push-up position but with your elbows on the ground and your weight on your forearms.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  3. Hold this position for as long as you can while breathing normally.
