Pull Up

Pull-ups strengthen the back, shoulders, and arms, and improve grip strength. They're performed by lifting your body up to a bar and lowering back down.

Steps to perform a Pull Up:

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with your hands shoulder-width apart, palms facing away from you.
  2. Pull yourself up until your chin is level with the bar, then slowly lower back down.
  3. Keep your core engaged and avoid swinging your legs.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
